# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Founders and developers who want to launch a production-ready SaaS quickly. They evaluate starter templates under time pressure and need confidence that core foundations (auth, billing, database, architecture) are already solved.

## Product Purpose
This starter kit helps teams ship a real Next.js SaaS baseline with less setup friction and fewer architectural mistakes. Success means visitors quickly understand credibility, scope, and implementation quality, then convert into trial users or code adopters.

## Brand Personality
Premium, calm, trustworthy. The voice should feel expert and practical, with clear proof over hype.

## Anti-references
- Dark neon or crypto-style aesthetics
- Overly playful consumer-app styling
- Gradient-heavy visual noise and decorative effects

## Design Principles
- Prove quality through clarity: show architecture and capability with concrete, scannable evidence.
- Reduce adoption anxiety: make setup and first steps feel straightforward and low-risk.
- Respect technical audiences: prioritize precision, structure, and signal over marketing fluff.
- Keep momentum visible: every major section should move users toward trying the starter.
- Communicate confidence quietly: premium craft, restrained motion, and purposeful contrast.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.2 AA as the baseline. Maintain high text contrast, clear focus states, keyboard accessibility, and semantic structure across marketing and app surfaces.
